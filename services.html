Goldilocks hair and beauty salon provides innovative hairdressing, beauty therapy, body massage, acupuncture and reflexology services.

We offer a number of services to enhance your image and well being. All staff members are fully qualified in their field of expertise and regularly update their skills.

Hair Care Products

Our experience has shown that no one product brand is suitable for everyone. We have introduced a wide range of brands to cater for all hair types. Goldilocks have a focus on environmental awareness. Our latest innovation has been to introduce water base and organic dyes, delighting both clientele and staff.

Beauty Services

We provide a wide range of beauty services including facials, manicures, pedicures and waxing.

Permanent Makeup

Permanent makeup is an alternative to applying your eye-liner, lip line and eyebrows daily. Our beauty therapist can provide advice on your specific needs.

Massage and Reflexology

Goldilocks provides massage, reflexology and aromatherapy services.
